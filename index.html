What VisionLore is

A modern, analytical approach to symbolism.

VisionLore is a structured, narrative-based framework used within The Lotus Oracle to support reflection, analysis, and perspective development.

It combines storytelling techniques, thematic exploration, and visual prompts to help individuals examine ideas, experiences, and patterns in an organized and thoughtful way.

The method is influenced by historical and cultural traditions of symbolism and meaning-making, including early Egyptian approaches, and is applied in a modern, analytical context.

VisionLore is not a psychic practice and does not involve prediction, supernatural claims, or intuitive abilities. Instead, it functions as a structured approach to exploring themes, concepts, and personal perspectives through guided discussion and narrative analysis.

Meet the founder

Jamilah Monique McCray

Jamilah is a writer, digital consultant, and content creator behind The Lotus Oracle, a platform focused on structured reflection, narrative exploration, and symbolic analysis.

She is the creator of the VisionLore method, a framework that uses storytelling techniques, thematic prompts, and visual concepts to help individuals examine ideas, experiences, and perspectives in an organized and thoughtful way.

Her work is influenced by historical and cultural traditions of symbolism and storytelling, including early Egyptian approaches to meaning-making and narrative structure. These influences are incorporated from an educational and conceptual standpoint, rather than as religious or predictive practice.

Jamilah approaches each session as a reflective and analytical process, encouraging clients to explore patterns, context, and personal interpretation through structured discussion and creative exploration.
